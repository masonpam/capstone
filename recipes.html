A summer treat Watermelon Jam

  • This jam is both savory and sweet with a little kick.
  • Ingredients:
  • Watermelon- Cut into small peices
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Black pepper
  • Balsamic glaze- Or balsamic reduction
  • Jalepeno- Remove seeds and chop
  • Lime juice
  • Agave- Just enough to sweeten it up a little
  • Brown sugar
  • Mint- Fresh mint is best

Put all ingredients into food processor and blend until combined.
Transfer to pan and boil until thick, stirring occasionally. Pairs great with Goat cheese!
