Moroccan Carrots

  • These carrots are bursting with unique flavors. Serve them warm or chilled as a side dish or on their own. This marinade recipe is good for about 3 batches of the carrots, and can store for up to a week and a half in the fridge.
  • Ingredients:
  • Large carrots- 8, washed
  • Red peppers- 2, roughly chopped
  • Shallot- 1, roughly chopped
  • Parsley leaves- 2 tablespoons
  • Red chile flakes- 1 teaspoon
  • Cinnamon- 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • Paprika- 1 teaspoon
  • Ground cumin- 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cayenne- 1 teaspoon
  • Salt- 1 tablespoon
  • Lemon juice- 1 teaspoon (or juice from 1 lemon)
  • Grapeseed oil- 1 cup (can subsitute canola or other neutral oil)
  • Water- 1/2 cup

Preheat oven to 425. Slice the carrots into quarters lengthwise, then into roughly 2"-long pieces. Place on a parchment or wax-paper lined cookie sheet.
For the marinade, blend all ingredients together in a blender or food processor until still slightly chunky. Pour 1 cup of the marinade over the carrots and mix until carrots are coated.
Roast for 8 minutes. carrots will still be slightly crispy
