Roasted Beet Muhammara

  • This is a great spread to serve with warm pita or dip fresh vegetables in.
  • Ingredients:
  • Large red beets- 6
  • Walnuts- 1 cup, toasted
  • Breadcrumbs- 1/2 cup
  • Balsamic vinegar- 2 tablespoons
  • Olive oil- 1/2 cup
  • Garlic cloves- 4, peeled
  • Salt- 2 teaspoons
  • Ground cumin- 2 teaspoons
  • Water- Just a splash

Preheat oven to 350. Cut the beets in half (do not peel) and lay over a large sheet of tin foil, careful not to poke any holes in the foil. Place on a cookie sheet and bake for one hour or until beets are fork tender. Unwrap beets and let sit for about 15 minutes.
Once the beets are cooled slightly, peel and cut into small chunks.
In a blender or food processor, add the beets and the rest of the ingredients. Blend until smooth. Add a little water to help blend more easily if necessary. Serve warm or chilled.

Muhammara originates from Aleppo, Syria
