Don't throw broccoli stems away!

Broccoli stems are great sliced up in stir fry, vegetable stock, diced up in soup or tossed into a summer corn saute.

Hardboiled eggs

Hardboiled eggs can be a pain to peel. My fool-proof trick is setting the timer for 10 minutes after the water starts boiling. When the eggs are done, quickly run them under cold water to cool down and then peel while still warm. Once peeled, continue to run under cold water to cool completely.

Cutting onions

Cold onions are less gaseous, keeping them in the fridge will help slow those fumes, and a super sharp knife cuts through cleanly and will help keep the tears away.
